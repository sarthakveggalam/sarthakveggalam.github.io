DailyatFive

Discover People who Love the Game Just like You.

    Connect people via local sports events

    Facilitate hosting and joining of sports events to connect people.

My Role

Lead User Research- Formulated questionnaire, Conducted Interviews.Literature Review. Concept Design.

Tools

Balsamiq Mockups. Adobe Illustrator. Paper Sketching.

Research

Conducted paper based survey and one on one interviews with 88 people.

Interviews were conducted in and around North Campus and India gate. Chosen primarily for an abundance of people within age group 18-30.

Objective was to get a deeper sense of what goes in the user’s psyche before he decide to play, what other tasks does he do while playing, what motivates him to play.

Formulated the questionnaire. You can see it here.

Findings

The main reasons people stopped playing was due to a shortage of venues as well as unavailability of active players.

About 80% of people used to play once in a week.Most of them wanted to play within a vicinity of 2kms

To organize a local sports event, most of them used messaging platforms like Whatsapp or used to call each other via mobile phones

Storyboard

Ram is sitting at home. He has recently shifted to a new city. Earlier, he used to play every weekend.Being a sports lover, he regularly indulged in sports activities to take his mind off work.However in his new place, he does not know anyone around. He goes on to the Play Store and downloads the app - ‘DailyatFive’

He opens the app and clicks on the Get Started button. Next, there are a series of steps he has to follow like allowing access to his location, choosing his favorite sport etc.

As he is done with all the steps he is shown various markers on a map which correspond to local sports events happening around him at that point of time. He clicks on the marker which shows information about the event i.e. Event Name, Host Name, address etc. There are also reviews associated with each event.

Ram can choose to join that event or go back and visit other sports events. He goes through the information presented and decides to join the event. A sign up dialog appears. He fills the form and then he is presented with a message dialogue box where he is asked to write a message to the host regarding his interest in joining.

After sending the message, he receives a notification that his request has been sent. About 5 mins later he receives a message from the host who is excited to see his interest.

In a matter of 30 mins, Ram was able to find people with similar sporting interests. Overjoyed and excited to meet his new friends he rushed to the spot and boy did he have a good time.
