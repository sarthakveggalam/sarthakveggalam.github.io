DailyatFive

Built with the intention to bring back a sort of quirkiness to the world of single page websites which are increasingly become mechanised and commonplace.

The website was designed keeping in mind the characteristics of the youth. A playful mix of illustrations and color was used to evoke a youthful exuberance urging people to go out and play.

A two person team. We decided to build a single page website to produce content with relative ease for the user. The content was iterated upon and developed in a fashion to appeal to the young.

Color was an essential component during the design process. Bold and bright colors were chosen to evoke the emotions of vigor and effervescence.

Enough talk. Scroll to see the website. Hope you like it.
